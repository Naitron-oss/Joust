How to Play

Bout is an endless tournament where knights battle until complete exhaustion! Best your enemies until you are finally bested yourself.

You are the white knight. Your enemies are red. Your steeds are enchanted. All of you can jump mid-air to your heart's content.

Meet them from above to send them plummeting to the ground.

Meet them head-on and you will bounce off of one another, unscathed.

Meet them from below and you will be bested — GAME OVER!

Controls

Jump: W / ↑ / Spacebar

Move left: A / ←

Move right: D / →

Click anywhere to pause.
