How to Play

You are the white knight. Your enemies are red. All of you can jump mid-air to your heart's content.

Meet them from above to send them plummeting to the ground.

Meet them head-on and you will bounce off of one another, unscathed.

Meet them from below and you will be bested — GAME OVER!

Run over your enemies' fallen steeds in time to deliver a coup de grace. Otherwise, they will regenerate!

Controls

Jump: W / ⇧ / Spacebar

Move left: A / ⇦

Move right: D / ⇨

Click anywhere to pause.
